About Me

I'm Shuvankar Debnath, a versatile creative professional with a diverse skill set spanning web development, graphic design, and video editing.

With a year of experience in web development, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have worked on projects like creating comprehensive survey forms with responsive designs. In the realm of graphic design, I have five months of experience and am skilled in Adobe Photoshop, Canva, and Adobe Illustrator.

My true passion lies in video editing, where I bring over five years of expertise using tools such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Wondershare Filmora, and Capcut. I have produced a wide range of videos, including vlogs, cinematic edits, and tutorials for YouTube and various clients.

Currently, I am pursuing a B.Tech degree, which I expect to complete by 2027. In addition to my professional endeavors, I have a keen interest in game development and continuously experiment with new techniques in video editing.
